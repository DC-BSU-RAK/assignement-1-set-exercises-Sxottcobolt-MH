A simple egg fried rice

This egg fried rice is a no-fuss meal, as it is easily customisable with any vegetables/meat you have at hand.

Preparation time

  • Total: 20 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 3 tablespoons peanut, vegatble, or olive oil
  • 1 cup diced onion(1 small onion)
  • 1 to 2 half cups mixed veggies, diced(bellpepper, carrots,etc)
  • half a cup of sliced scallions(Have the white and green parts seprated)
  • 4 cups cooked overnight jasmine rice
  • 2 half teaspoons of soy sauce
  • half teaspoon of five-spice powder
  • 1 to 2 teaspoons toasted sesame oil

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a small bowl and beat them together.
  2. Heat a skillet with 1 tablespoon of the oil over medium-high heat. Once the pan is hot, add the beaten eggs and scramble them for about a minute. Transfer the eggs to a dish and turn off the heat.
  3. Drizzle the remaining 2 tablespoons of oil into a wok over high heat. Add the onions and cook them for about 1 to 2 minutes, stirring constantly. Add the mixed veggies and white parts of the scallions and cook for 2 minutes. Then season the veggies with a pinch of salt.
  4. Add the cooked rice into a pan or wok, and cook for several minutes, until the rice is heated through. If there're large clumps of rice, break them apart with the back of your spatula.
  5. Add the soy sauce, five-spice powder, dash of white pepper and sesame olive oil and stir to distribute the seasonings. Then add the scrambled eggs and stir to mix again. And then garnish it with the remaining sliced scallions.
